Our Global Impact

Creating positive change across continents through responsible IT recycling

Renvia IT Ltd is part of a wider circular technology ecosystem. Through our global impact arm at Renvia IT (renviait.org) and our Africa-based initiative Renvia Tech Hub Africa, suitable refurbished devices from our UK collections are routed into schools, youth programmes and community projects across Nigeria and Africa.

This means that when you choose Renvia for IT collections, you are not only complying with UK waste and data regulations – you are also directly supporting digital inclusion, education and future tech talent in underserved communities.

Why IT Recycling Matters

Understanding the environmental benefits of responsible e-waste disposal

The E-Waste Challenge

Over 50 million tonnes of e-waste are generated globally each year, with only 20% being formally recycled. The rest ends up in landfills, releasing toxic materials into the environment.

Data Security

Professional recycling ensures your sensitive data is completely destroyed using certified methods, protecting your business from data breaches and ensuring compliance with regulations.

Circular Economy

Recycling recovers valuable materials like gold, silver, and copper, reducing the need for mining and creating a sustainable cycle that benefits both business and the environment.
