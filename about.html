About Renvia IT

Giving enterprise technology a second life through secure, sustainable reuse

Who We Are

Renvia IT is a UK-based IT asset recycling and refurbishment company focused on secure, compliant, and environmentally responsible disposal of enterprise technology.

We help businesses, public sector organisations, and schools turn unused or end-of-life IT equipment into secure, trackable, and measurable impact — for both the planet and their bottom line.

Our Mission

To simplify sustainable IT disposal by combining enterprise-grade data security, transparent reporting, and circular economy principles.

  • Zero-landfill approach to e-waste
  • Fully auditable, GDPR-compliant processes
  • Real, measurable ESG impact for our clients

Our Values

The principles that guide every collection, every device, and every decision

Security First

We treat every device as if it contains critical data, ensuring certified destruction and full audit trails.

Sustainable by Design

We prioritise reuse, refurbishment, and component recovery before recycling, maximising value and impact.

Transparent & Compliant

From chain-of-custody logs to certificates of destruction, you always know where your assets are and what’s happened to them.

People & Planet

We work with schools, communities, and partners to ensure technology reaches those who need it most.

Built for Modern IT & ESG Teams

Combining operational excellence with sustainability and compliance expertise

Enterprise-Grade Processes

Designed for IT managers, procurement teams, and sustainability leaders who need documented, repeatable processes at scale.

Certified & Registered

Fully registered UK business with all relevant waste, data, and environmental certifications and registrations.

Partnership Approach

We work as a long-term partner, not just a one-off collector — helping you plan, track, and report your IT asset lifecycle.
