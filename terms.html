Service Overview

1 Service Description

Renvia IT provides professional IT recycling services including but not limited to:

  • Secure collection of IT equipment from client premises
  • Data destruction and sanitization services
  • IT asset recycling and refurbishment
  • Compliance documentation and reporting
  • Environmental disposal of non-reusable equipment

2 Service Levels

We offer two primary service levels:

Service Level Includes Documentation
Standard Service Basic collection, data destruction, recycling Batch certificates of destruction
Enhanced Service Item-level processing, detailed reporting Individual asset certificates, ESG reports

Client Responsibilities

3 Equipment Preparation

Clients are responsible for:

  • Providing accurate equipment lists and descriptions
  • Ensuring equipment is accessible at the scheduled collection time
  • Notifying us of any hazardous materials or special handling requirements
  • Backing up any data they wish to retain before collection

4 Site Access & Safety

Clients must provide:

  • Safe and clear access to equipment locations
  • Relevant health and safety information for the site
  • Any necessary security clearance for our personnel
  • Adequate parking and loading facilities

Warning: Failure to provide safe access or accurate equipment information may result in additional charges or service cancellation.

Data Destruction & Security

5 Data Handling

We implement industry-standard data destruction methods aligned with GDPR requirements and recognized wiping protocols including NIST and DoD standards.

6 Destruction Methods

  • Software Wiping: For reusable devices using certified data erasure software
  • Physical Destruction: For high-risk media or non-functional devices
  • Degaussing: For magnetic media where appropriate

7 Certificates

Certificates of Destruction are provided upon completion of services. Standard service includes batch certificates, while enhanced service includes individual asset certificates.
