Your Donation Journey

A seamless process from collection to measurable community impact

1

Schedule Free Collection

To arrange your donation, please complete our online booking form. Simply title the collection 'Social Impact Donation' in the equipment details section. We will schedule a convenient collection time for you completely free of charge. All donated equipment will undergo our Certified Data Destruction process before reuse.

2

Secure Data Handling

All data-bearing devices undergo professional secure wiping following industry best practices. Optional certified data destruction certificates are available for organizations requiring formal documentation.

3

Assessment & Refurbishment

Our technicians evaluate, test, and prepare equipment for reuse. Functional devices are refurbished; non-working items are responsibly recycled with parts recovered for repairs.

4

Deployment to Tech Hubs

Equipment is shipped to our vetted partner tech education centers in Nigeria and across Africa, where it directly supports hands-on training programs and digital literacy initiatives.

5

Impact Reporting

Receive detailed documentation showing device counts, environmental metrics (CO₂e savings), and the social impact of your contribution—perfect for stakeholder communications and annual reports.
