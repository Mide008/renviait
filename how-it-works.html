How It Works

A simple, secure, and sustainable IT asset disposal process from start to finish

1

Book Your Collection

Use our online booking form or contact our team. Choose a date and time that works for your organisation. We confirm everything with you in writing.

  • Free, no-obligation quote
  • Flexible scheduling
  • Instant email confirmation
2

Secure On-Site Collection

Our trained, uniformed team arrives with all necessary equipment. We manage loading, inventory, and documentation while minimising disruption.

  • Full chain-of-custody tracking
  • Liability-insured logistics
  • Detailed asset inventory
3

Certified Data Destruction

Storage devices are sanitised or physically destroyed using industry-standard methods. Every action is logged and mapped to your assets.

  • NIST- & DoD-aligned data wiping
  • Physical destruction options
  • Certificates of destruction issued
4

Refurbish, Reuse, or Recycle

Devices are tested, graded, and either refurbished for reuse, harvested for parts, or recycled through approved downstream partners.

  • Reuse first, recycle as last resort
  • 98% diversion from landfill (target)
  • Responsible material recovery
5

Reporting & ESG Documentation

You receive a complete documentation pack outlining asset disposition, data destruction, compliance, and environmental impact.

  • Certificates and waste transfer notes
  • ESG and carbon impact summaries
  • Audit-ready documentation for your records
