How It Works

A simple, secure and sustainable process for collecting, wiping and reusing your retired IT equipment.

1

Book Your Collection

Tell us what equipment you're disposing of, where it's located, and when you'd like us to attend.

  • Free, no-obligation quote
  • Flexible scheduling around your business hours
  • Clear confirmation of what we will collect
  • Simple instructions for preparing your site (if required)
2

Secure On-Site Collection

Our trained team arrives with tamper-evident containers and the correct handling equipment. We manage all loading and verification on-site.

  • Full chain-of-custody from your premises to our facility
  • Insured logistics and professional crew
  • Disruption-free loading
  • Asset list captured where applicable
3

Certified Data Destruction

All data-bearing devices are sanitised or destroyed using industry-standard methods that align with GDPR and recognised wiping protocols.

  • NIST/DoD-aligned data wiping for reusable devices
  • Physical destruction options for high-risk media
  • Every action logged for your compliance records
  • Certificates of destruction provided (standard or enhanced depending on requirements)
4

Refurbish, Reuse or Recycle

We prioritise reuse to maximise asset life and minimise waste. Anything non-reusable is responsibly dismantled or recycled through approved partners.

  • "Reuse first" sustainability approach
  • High diversion from landfill
  • Approved downstream recycling partners
  • Responsible recovery of materials
5

Reporting & ESG Documentation

You receive a clear set of documents to support internal reporting, audits and compliance checks.

  • Certificates of data destruction
  • Summary of collected items
  • Documentation suitable for internal or external audits
  • Optional ESG and carbon reporting
