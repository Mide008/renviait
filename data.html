Certified Data Destruction Methods

Industry-standard approaches tailored to your security requirements and compliance needs

Software Data Wiping

Secure erasure using certified software that overwrites data multiple times, making recovery impossible.

  • NIST 800-88 compliant
  • DoD 5220.22-M standards
  • Multiple pass overwriting
  • Ideal for reusable devices
  • Full verification reports

Physical Destruction

Complete physical destruction of storage media ensuring absolute data security for high-risk materials.

  • Hard drive shredding
  • SSD crushing technology
  • Degaussing for magnetic media
  • Particle size certification
  • Video evidence available

Degaussing

Powerful magnetic field erasure for traditional magnetic media, permanently destroying data patterns.

  • High-strength degaussing
  • Ideal for tapes & floppies
  • Instant data destruction
  • No heat or debris
  • Media reuse prevention
