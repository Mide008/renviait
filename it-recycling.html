Our Recycling Process

A streamlined workflow ensuring security, compliance, and environmental responsibility

1

Assessment & Booking

We discuss your requirements, provide a transparent quote, and schedule collection at your convenience.

2

Secure Collection

Our trained team collects equipment with full chain of custody documentation and secure transportation.

3

Data Destruction

All data-bearing devices undergo certified wiping or physical destruction with full audit trail.

4

Equipment Processing

Devices are tested, refurbished for reuse, or responsibly dismantled for material recovery.

5

Material Recycling

Components are separated and sent to approved partners for responsible material recycling.

6

Reporting & Certification

You receive comprehensive documentation including certificates of destruction and environmental reports.
