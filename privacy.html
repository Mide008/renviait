1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Renvia IT Ltd (“Renvia”, “we”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects personal data in connection with our website, collection booking services, and related operations.

We are committed to handling your personal data in accordance with UK data protection law, including the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act.

2. Data Controller

Renvia IT Ltd is the data controller for personal data processed in connection with our services and this website.

If you have any questions regarding this Policy or how we use your data, please contact us using the details in the Contact & Complaints section.

3. Data We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Contact details: name, email address, phone number, job title.
  • Organisation details: company name, address, department.
  • Service information: details of your collection requests, quotes, and communications.
  • Website usage data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and interactions with forms.
  • Marketing preferences: your choices regarding receiving updates from us.

4. How We Use Your Data

We use personal data for the following purposes:

  • To respond to enquiries and provide quotes or proposals.
  • To manage bookings, collections, and service delivery.
  • To issue documentation, certificates, and reports.
  • To improve our website, services, and user experience.
  • To comply with legal, regulatory, and audit requirements.
  • To send relevant updates or marketing communications, where permitted.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Contract: where processing is necessary to provide our services or take steps at your request.
  • Legal obligation: where we must maintain records or documentation for regulatory purposes.
  • Legitimate interests: for business operations, service improvement, and security.
  • Consent: for optional marketing communications or non-essential cookies, where required.

6. Sharing Your Data

We may share personal data with trusted third parties where necessary for service delivery, such as logistics providers, IT partners, or email service platforms.

We do not sell your personal data. Any third parties we work with are required to keep your data secure and use it only in accordance with our instructions and applicable law.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes we collected it for, including satisfying legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.

Retention periods may vary depending on the type of data and the context of our relationship with you (for example, ongoing contracts vs. one-off enquiries).

8. Data Security

We use a combination of technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

While we take reasonable steps to secure your data, no system can be completely secure. You are also responsible for keeping your own login details confidential where applicable.

9. Your Rights

Under data protection law, you may have the following rights:

  • To request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • To request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • To request deletion of your data in certain circumstances.
  • To object to or restrict our processing in certain situations.
  • To withdraw consent where processing is based on your consent.
  • To lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

10. Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve functionality, analyse usage, and support certain features (such as forms or embedded content).

Where required by law, we will ask for your consent before using non-essential cookies. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings or our cookie banner (where available).

11. Contact & Complaints

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact:

  • Email (General): info@renviait.co.uk
  • Email (Support): support@renviait.co.uk
  • Email (Legal): admin@renviait.co.uk

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority if you believe your rights have been violated.
